About LUNA SEN Insurance

LUNA SEN Insurance is a specialized insurance provider dedicated to serving SEN (Special Educational Needs) learners, disabled learners, and individuals with EHCP (Education, Health and Care Plans). We understand the unique needs of learners aged 16 and above who require specialized insurance coverage.

Our mission is to provide comprehensive insurance solutions that support learners in their educational journey and help them become their own boss. Whether you're a LUNA member or a SEN/disabled learner, we offer tailored insurance products designed to meet your specific requirements.

We specialize in insurance for qualifications, certifications, and educational pursuits, ensuring that all learners have access to the protection they need to succeed in their chosen paths.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is LUNA SEN Insurance? +
LUNA SEN Insurance provides specialized insurance coverage for SEN (Special Educational Needs) learners, disabled learners, and individuals with EHCP (Education, Health and Care Plans) aged 16 and above.
Who is eligible for LUNA SEN Insurance? +
Our insurance is available for SEN learners, disabled learners, and EHCP holders aged 16+. Whether you're a LUNA member or a SEN/disabled learner, we offer tailored insurance products to meet your specific requirements.
What types of insurance do you offer? +
We specialize in insurance for qualifications, certifications, and educational pursuits. Our products are designed to support learners in their educational journey and help them become their own boss.
How do I get a quotation? +
You can get a personalized quotation by selecting the appropriate option on our homepage - either as a LUNA learner or as a SEN/Disabled learner. Our team will provide you with a tailored insurance solution.
What is an EHCP and do I need one? +
An EHCP (Education, Health and Care Plan) is a legal document that describes a child or young person's special educational, health, and social care needs. While having an EHCP can help identify your needs, it's not always required for our insurance products.
How can I contact LUNA SEN Insurance? +
You can contact us through our contact section on this page. We're here to help with any questions about our services, quotations, or to discuss your specific insurance needs for your educational journey.
